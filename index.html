Introduction:

One of the most controversial topics in the very young generation of the world is the debate between which comic is better: DC or Marvel! There are many ways to compare the two from the comic books to the movies to the character themselves. The comparisons themselves yield many results and that itself can be a debatable topic. For this assignment, I have chosen movies to compare the two. I have taken data of the movies from both the franchise from the year 2008 to 2019. The movies that are included as a part of the dataset are only the ones that belong in the respective universes, i.e. each of the movies within the franchise, is a part of the same universe and their characters and storylines have crossovers. I have taken the data from the following website: https://www.kaggle.com/leonardopena/marvel-vs-dc. I have used Python along with Pandas library to process the data according to my usecase. I am using the IMDB ratings, Gross revenue and the budget of the movies to compare and show you the results. We can also hover over th points in the chart to see more details about the points/bars that are plotted.

Left Chart:

The left chart shows you a scatter plot showing the budget of the movies plotted agains their respective IMDB ratings. The plot itself has been divided into four quadrants based on the average budget of the movies ($18M) and a preferred IMDB rating of movies. From the graph, we can infer that DC has highest rated movies compared to Marvel. Apart from that information, we can also see that DC has a lower budget for a movie on an average and also is very performant in the production of the movie withing that budget as we can see from the left-top quadrant, DC has created the best movies that has better IMDB ratings at a much lower budget compared to Marvel. Marvel has also produced very good movies with IMDB rating above 8.0 but the cost of its production is also significantly high (especially for Endgame and Infinity War). Hence the left chart is pro-DC.

Right Chart:

The chart on the right shows the Gross return for the movies in Millions of Dollars across the globe. Clearly from that graph we can see that not only Marvel has produced more number of movies in the given years, but also their average returns is a lot higher in value compared to DC. This shows that the money they invest that we can see from the left chart, is being returned in a profitable fashion. Hence this part is pro-Marvel.

Personal Opinion:

My personal opinion is that Marvel is the winner when it comes to comparisons with movies. This is because they have been consistent in terms of the IMDB ratings even if they dont have movies that are as good as the best ones in DC.
